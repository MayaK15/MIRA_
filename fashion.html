Spring Fashion

Spring Trends Straight Off The Runway April 17

FLORAL IS BACK

Florals are back once again, proving their timelessness on the runways. From delicate daisies to bold botanical prints, designers are embracing nature’s patterns to celebrate the return of spring.

STATEMENT SKIRTS

Tailored and effortless, blazer sets are the ultimate go-to this spring. Whether monochrome or mixed with playful prints, they’re redefining power dressing for the season.

BOHO CHIC

The boho spirit is back, led by designers like Isabel Marant who are reviving fringe, lace, and layered textures. It's all about relaxed silhouettes and carefree elegance.

Resort Wear You NEED

RESORTWEAR 2025

Time to get your linen sets out of the back of your closet, it's summer time soon! New additions to your resort wear collection should include crochet cover ups, missoni and pucci sets, and a nice hat to cover your face as you tan. Don't forget your SPF!

Where to Buy Vintage Jeans For The Spring

VINTAGE JEAN SHOPPING

For my NYC girlies, the best stores to find vintage jeans are Reformation's vintage location in the lower east side and What Comes Around Goes Around vintage shop. For my non-local girlies, second hand stores like Etsy, Poshmark, and Depop are your best friend this season!
